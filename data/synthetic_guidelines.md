## Hypertension management
First-line therapy for stage 1 hypertension in adults without comorbidities
includes thiazide diuretics, ACE inhibitors, ARBs, or calcium channel blockers.
Target blood pressure is generally below 130/80 mmHg for most adults.

## Type 2 diabetes
Metformin is the preferred initial pharmacologic agent for type 2 diabetes
unless contraindicated. Target HbA1c is typically below 7% for many nonpregnant
adults, individualized by patient factors.

## Sepsis screening
Initiate sepsis evaluation when two or more SIRS criteria are present with
suspected infection. Obtain lactate, blood cultures, and begin broad-spectrum
antibiotics within one hour of recognition for septic shock.
